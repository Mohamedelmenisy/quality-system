Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? You might find your answer here.

How can I see my mistakes?

After logging in, you'll find a "My Tickets" section that displays all errors registered under your name with full details.

Can I dispute a registered error?

Yes, each ticket includes a "Dispute" option, which is sent to your manager and the quality team for review.

How is the quality score calculated?

It's calculated based on a formula that considers the number of correct orders out of the total reviewed, with different weights for error types.
