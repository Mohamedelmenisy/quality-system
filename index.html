Frequently Asked Questions

How can I see my mistakes?

After logging in, you'll find a dedicated dashboard that displays all errors registered under your name with full details and feedback from the quality team.

Is it possible to dispute a registered error?

Yes, the system will include a feature to raise a dispute on a ticket. This action will notify your manager and the quality team for a re-evaluation.

How is the overall quality score calculated?

The quality score is a percentage based on the number of correct reviews versus the total number of reviews. Different types of errors may have different weights to reflect their severity.
