Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a new review?

Click the "+ New Review" button in the top-right corner of the dashboard. This will open the review interface where you can input the order details.

Where can I see my assigned orders?

Navigate to the "My Assigned Orders" tab from the left sidebar. You can filter and search for specific orders there.

How is the Quality Score calculated?

The Average Quality Score is calculated based on the ratio of "Correct Decisions" to the total number of reviews completed in the selected time frame.
